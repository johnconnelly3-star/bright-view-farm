Eclipse Award  ·  January 2026

Book'em Danno

2025 Eclipse Award — Champion Male Sprinter

In January 2026, at the Eclipse Awards ceremony in Florida, Book'em Danno was named the 2025 Eclipse Award winner for Champion Male Sprinter — the highest individual honor in American Thoroughbred racing, presented annually by the National Thoroughbred Racing Association, Thoroughbred Racing Associations, and the National Turf Writers and Broadcasters.

Bright View Farm co-bred Book'em Danno, and his Eclipse Award stands as the crowning achievement in Christine Connelly's fifty years of dedication to the Thoroughbred. It is a testament to what a committed family breeding operation — patient, principled, and passionate — can produce.

The Eclipse Awards are named in honor of the great 18th-century racehorse Eclipse, who was undefeated in 18 starts and went on to sire an extraordinary number of champions. To win one is to be recognized among the very best the sport has to offer in a given year.

Thoroughbred Owners & Breeders Association

TOBA New Jersey Breeder of the Year

The TOBA New Jersey Breeder of the Year award recognizes the state's top Thoroughbred breeding operation for results on the track and at auction. Bright View Farm has earned this distinction four times, most recently in three consecutive years.

2024

TOBA NJ Breeder of the Year

The most recent recognition — the third consecutive year Bright View Farm received this honor — confirming the farm's sustained excellence at the highest level of New Jersey Thoroughbred breeding.

2023

TOBA NJ Breeder of the Year

A second consecutive year earning New Jersey's top breeding award, reflecting continued strength from Bright View Farm's homebred and co-bred Thoroughbreds on the track and in the sales ring.

2022

TOBA NJ Breeder of the Year

The beginning of a remarkable three-year run of recognition, affirming Bright View Farm's place as New Jersey's leading Thoroughbred breeding operation during this period.

2011

TOBA NJ Breeder of the Year

Bright View Farm's first TOBA New Jersey Breeder of the Year honor, awarded over a decade before the recent streak — evidence of a long and distinguished breeding record spanning multiple eras.

Notable Racing Results

January 2026  ·  Eclipse Awards, Florida

Book'em Danno Named 2025 Eclipse Award Champion Male Sprinter

Co-bred by Bright View Farm, Book'em Danno earned the sport's highest individual honor at the 2026 Eclipse Awards ceremony. His season-long dominance in sprint races was recognized by a vote of the NTRA, TRA, and NTWAB, placing him at the pinnacle of American Thoroughbred racing for 2025.

November 4, 2013  ·  Golden Gate Fields

Exit Stage Left Wins the Golden Nugget Stakes on Debut

Bright View Farm homebred Exit Stage Left, a chestnut colt, broke his maiden in style with a first-out victory in the Golden Nugget Stakes at Golden Gate Fields. A promising beginning for a colt bred and raised on the farm's 270-acre Chesterfield property.
