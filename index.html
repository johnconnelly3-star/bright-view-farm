Bright View Farm is a full-service Thoroughbred breeding and boarding operation on 270 acres in Chesterfield, New Jersey. Founded in 1975, the farm specializes in the care of mares, foals, weanlings, and yearlings — raising horses for both racing and sales with consistent, unhurried attention.

Six-time TOBA New Jersey Breeder of the Year — 1986, 2011, 2014, 2022, 2023, and 2024 — and home of Book'em Danno, the 2025 Eclipse Award Champion Male Sprinter.
