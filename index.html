About the Farm

One of New Jersey's Last Thoroughbred Nurseries

Bright View Farm is a full-service Thoroughbred breeding farm situated on 270 acres of prime, lush pastureland in Chesterfield, New Jersey. Founded in 1975 by Philadelphia businessman John Connelly, the farm has been guided by the same commitment to excellence for half a century.

Today, under the stewardship of Christine Connelly, Bright View Farm remains one of the last operating Thoroughbred nurseries in New Jersey — breeding horses for both the racing circuit and the auction ring, and offering world-class care for mares, weanlings, and yearlings.
