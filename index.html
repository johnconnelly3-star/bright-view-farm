Bright View Farm is a full-service Thoroughbred breeding and boarding operation on 270 acres in Chesterfield, New Jersey. Founded in 1975, the farm specializes in the care of mares, foals, weanlings, and yearlings — raising horses for both racing and sales with the kind of consistent, unhurried attention that has made it one of the most trusted operations in the region. Visitors often remark that it looks like a piece of Kentucky.

Six-time TOBA New Jersey Breeder of the Year — 1986, 2011, 2014, 2022, 2023, and 2024 — and home of Book'em Danno, the 2025 Eclipse Award Champion Male Sprinter.
