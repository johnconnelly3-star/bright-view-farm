Driving Directions

From the New Jersey Turnpike, take Exit 7 (Bordentown) and merge onto Route 206 North. At the second traffic light, turn right onto Georgetown Road (Route 545 South). After 6.5 miles, you will see the black fences of Bright View Farm on the left.
