Our Story

Bright View Farm has the look and feel of Kentucky horse country — carefully maintained fields, pastures tested annually for nutritional content, and a 1736 farmhouse at its center that has stood for nearly three centuries.

One of the last remaining Thoroughbred nurseries in New Jersey, Bright View breeds horses for both racing and sales. Clients are owners and breeders who trust the farm to give their horses the time, space, and expertise the work demands.
