Our Story

Bright View Farm has operated on 270 acres in Chesterfield, New Jersey since 1975. Owned by Christine Connelly, the farm has built a reputation over five decades for high standards, careful management, and results — earning TOBA New Jersey Breeder of the Year honors in 1986, 2011, 2014, 2022, 2023, and 2024.

In January 2026, Book'em Danno, co-bred by Bright View Farm, was named the 2025 Eclipse Award Champion Male Sprinter.

One of the last remaining Thoroughbred nurseries in New Jersey, Bright View breeds horses for both racing and sales. Clients are owners and breeders who trust the farm to give their horses the time, space, and expertise the work demands.
