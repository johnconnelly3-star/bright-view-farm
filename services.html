Bright View Farm offers year-round and seasonal boarding for mares, weanlings, and yearlings on 270 acres of carefully maintained pastureland in Chesterfield, New Jersey — 45 minutes from Philadelphia and Monmouth Racetrack, and 90 minutes from New York City.

The farm provides lay-up and rehabilitation for horses recovering from surgery or injury, working closely with your veterinarian to follow prescribed protocols. Sales preparation is also available for yearlings heading to auction, with a focus on condition, manners, and presentation.

Veterinary care is coordinated on behalf of clients through long-standing relationships with experienced equine veterinarians in the region, covering routine wellness, reproductive management, and specialist consultations.
