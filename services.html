Bright View Farm offers a range of services for mares, weanlings, and yearlings, located 45 minutes from Philadelphia and Monmouth Racetrack, and 90 minutes from New York City:

  • Year-round and seasonal boarding
  • Lay-up and rehabilitation for post-operative horses
  • Coordinated veterinary care through long-standing relationships with experienced equine veterinarians

The farm works closely with each client's veterinarian to follow prescribed protocols and provides routine wellness, reproductive management, and specialist consultations.
